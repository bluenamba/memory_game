INSTRUCTIONS:
1. START BY FLIPPING A CARD.

4. IF THE CARD'S IMAGE ARE DIFFERENT,
BOTH CARDS WILL FACE DOWNWARDS AGAIN.

6. CAUTION: YOU HAVE TO FLIP YOUR CARD WITHIN THE GIVEN NUMBER OF MOVES!!
7. ONCE ALL THE CARDS ARE FLIPPED WITH THEIR MATCH,
YOU HAVE WON! AND, MAY PROCEED TO THE NEXT LEVEL.
