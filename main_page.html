Welcome to Brain Train

THIS GAME WILL HELP YOU IMPROVE YOUR MEMORY RECALL SKILLS.
THE INSTRUCTIONS ARE SIMPLE TO FOLLOW
INSTRUCTIONS:
1. START BY FLIPPING A CARD.
2. FLIP ANOTHER CARD OF YOUR CHOICE.
3. IF THE CARDS' IMAGES ARE SIMILAR,
YOU FOUND YOUR FIRST MATCH!
4. IF THE CARD'S IMAGE ARE DIFFERENT,
BOTH CARDS WILL FACE DOWNWARDS AGAIN.
5. YOU CAN FLIP ANOTHER CARD TO FIND ITS MATCH.
6. ONCE ALL THE CARDS ARE FLIPPED WITH THEIR MATCH,
YOU HAVE WON! AND, MAY PROCEED TO THE NEXT LEVEL.

CAUTION: YOU HAVE TO FLIP YOUR CARD WITHIN THE GIVEN NUMBER OF MOVES!!
FAILURE TO DO SO, WILL START YOUR CURRENT LEVEL AGAIN!
